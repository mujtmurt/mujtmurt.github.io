ABOUT

WEB & INTERACTION DESIGN CONFERENCE.

The W.ID Conference is a two-day, single track conference centered around the practice of Web & Interaction Design with tools such as Adobe Experience Design, which is also known as Adobe Xd, and Php Storm. In 2018, we are following the main program by educational, hands-on workshops. Our conference program merges latest in the Web and Interaction Designr research, technology and practice, constructed by an open-call submission and committee review process.

Running from Indy since 2010, W.ID is proud to be taking its next conference in Europe in 2019. Join us in Germany to learn about web & interaction design using the best programs we have to offer (hopefully) in 2019!

W.ID Conference will be taking place on December 23rd to 25th at the JW Marriott Hotel in Indianapolis. Workshops (a seperate registration) will be held on December 26th.

WANT TO BE A SPEAKER?

THIS IS YOUR CHANCE.

At W.ID, the 2-day single-track program is constructed from submissions from you, after careful program committee review. Our call for speakers is open through August 15th, 2018 and is open to anyone with a compelling story to tell about the practice of web and interaction design. We welcome speakers from the industry and academia, technologists and artists, students and teachers alike.

We are also seeking workshop presenters. Workshops will be run the day after the main conference. Accepted workshop givers will recieve a free registration to the main conference.
