about us

Double Tap Engineering is a family owned and operated machine shop with over 40 years experience in the machining industry.  We are passionate about our work, pride ourselves in being excellent problem-solvers, and are dedicated to superior quality.

Double Tap Engineering is located just minutes from I-69 between Anderson and Muncie, IN.  We strive to reduce lead times and deliver quality parts to our customers on time, every time.    

For more information on the services we provide or to discuss your next project, please contact us via email or phone.  We look forward to helping you and your business succeed. 

owners

mike stephenson:

In 1988 Mike became a Journeyman Tool & Die Maker and shortly after began working for General Motors.  Over the next 30 years Mike worked for General Motors in a variety of roles.  Those roles include tool and die making, troubleshooting, and CNC machining.  Mike is proficient in MIG and TIG welding and is part owner of Double Tap Engineering.

cody stephenson:

Cody is a third generation machinist and a graduate of Vincennes and Purdue University.  Cody was first introduced to machining in high school while attending John Hind’s Career Center’s Machine Trades program.  He then studied precision machining at Vincennes University, with a focus in Injection Mold Tooling.  After graduating from VU, he obtained his bachelor’s degree through Purdue University’s Industrial Technology program.  Cody worked as a Manufacturing Engineer for SMC Corp. prior to setting out on a new journey, as part owner of Double Tap Engineering.
